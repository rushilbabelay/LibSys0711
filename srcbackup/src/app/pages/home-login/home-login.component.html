Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi is the 14th Prime Minister of India. He is known for his dynamic, dedicated and determined nature. He has written several books including Social Harmony, Sakshi Bhav, Abode of Love, Jyotipunj, and Letters to Mother.

Chetan Bhagat

Chetan Bhagat is an author, columnist, and YouTuber. He was listed in Time magazine’s list of World’s 100 Most Influential People in 2010. Five of his novels have been adapted into films.

William Shakespeare

William Shakespeare was an English playwright, poet, and actor. He is often called England’s national poet and the “Bard of Avon”. He is regarded as the greatest writer in the English language and the world’s pre-eminent dramatist.
